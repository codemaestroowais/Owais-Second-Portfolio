About Me

I am a Computer Science student at UET Lahore with a strong passion for software development, AI, and cybersecurity. Beyond my coursework, I have spent a lot of time building real-world projects, such as Chrome extensions, database management systems, AI-powered detection systems, and even a project where I implemented IP banning to block brute force attacks and a DSA learning platform. I mostly work on front-end development because I really enjoy building easy-to-use and good-looking websites.

I specialize in front-end development and WordPress. I focus on making interfaces that are not just beautiful, but also easy to use and optimized for performance.

I enjoy working with C++, C#, Python, and JavaScript (React). I like writing clean and simple code and making designs that are easy for people to use. I have also tried building network tools and Chrome extensions along the way.

Right now, I am learning more about AI, front-end development, and cybersecurity. I am excited to use my skills in real projects and keep learning from experienced people in the field.
